TechHire Initiative

In March of 2015 the White House launched the TechHire Initiative, a Multi-Sector Effort and Call to Action to Give Americans Pathways to Well-Paying Technology Jobs. The core focus of this initiative is to serve the needs of young adults with barriers to training and employment. 


Through the ongoing leadership of CareerSource South Florida combined with innovative new training and employer engagement models, we now have the ability to provide our community with greater opportunity for in demand technology careers.
