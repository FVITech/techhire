TechHire Summer Bootcamp

The TechHire Bootcamp is six weeks and upon completion, the participant will receive an industry recognized credential. The 2020 Summer Bootcamp will offer its participants one of the following courses: Web Development, Cybersecurity, Gaming, Web Applications, Comp TIA A+ and Networking.
