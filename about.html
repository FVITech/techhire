Who We Are

Our FIVE core principles for success: Building a Sustainable Platform, Ensuring Measurable Outcomes, Creating Community and Employer Engagement, Focusing on Technology Skills and Careers, and Reducing Barriers to Training and Employment.

Our Partners

CareerSource South Florida, Florida Vocational Institute, Wyncode, The Idea Center at Miami-Dade College, Launch Code, The Beacon Council Foundation, Miami-Dade County, Iron Hack, New Horizons South Florida, Code Fever, The Academy, and TechLaunch.

1

Building a Sustainable Platform

We are focused on building a coalition of partners that will allow our community to closely align skill and technology advancement with successful career paths.

2

Ensuring Measurable Outcomes

Through the CareerSource system we will measure the impact of our program on the long term increase in income that the students entering careers will earn.

3

Creating Community and Employer Engagement

We drive to keep the career preparation of our students aligned with the current needs of local employers.

4

Focusing on Technology Skills and Careers

Creating a focus on in-demand skills emphasizing short term, high impact career training supported by a core group of Miami's training companies.

5

Reducing Barriers to Training and Employment

Our ultimate goal is to establish systems and relationships that help reduce inhibitions to career success.
